# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU) / LETTER OF INTENT (LOI)

**BETWEEN:**
Swift Spark Studios Ltd. ("The Provider")
**AND:**
Kingswood Interior Ltd. ("The Pilot Partner")

**1. PURPOSE**
The parties intend to collaborate on a Pilot Project to implement and test a Drawing Version Control Software ("The Software") designed to reduce versioning errors and improve communication between the shop floor and installation sites.

**2. SCOPE OF PILOT**
- **Duration:** 6 months (May 2026 - October 2026).
- **Users:** Up to 300 shop workers and 50 site installers.
- **Hardware:** The Pilot Partner will utilize their own existing mobile devices or tablets to access the Software. Swift Spark Studios will provide technical support for device compatibility.

**3. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES**
- **Swift Spark Studios:** Provide software access, training, and technical support.
- **The Pilot Partner:** Provide feedback on usability, report bugs, and allow the capture of "Before/After" efficiency metrics for grant reporting purposes.

**4. FUNDING COLLABORATION**
The Pilot Partner agrees to be named as the "Pilot Site" for Swift Spark Studios' application for the **Alberta Innovates Product Demo Program (PDP)** and other relevant government subsidies. There is no direct financial cost to the Pilot Partner for participating in this funded pilot.

**5. CONFIDENTIALITY**
Both parties agree to protect proprietary technical and business information shared during the pilot.
