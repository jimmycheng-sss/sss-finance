# Swift Spark Studios - Subsidy & Grant Strategy

## 1. Top Priority: R&D & Talent
- **NRC IRAP:** Direct funding for innovative tech development. Requires meeting with an ITA.
- **SR&ED Tax Credit:** 35% - 60% cash back on R&D wages. *Crucial: Maintain daily dev logs.*
- **MITACS:** Funding for hiring interns (Post-grad/PhD) for specific R&D projects.

## 2. Alberta Specific Grants
- **Alberta Innovates - Product Demo Program:** Helps with the cost of a pilot project with a real customer.
- **Jobs and Economy Grant:** Funding for hiring new Albertans.

## 3. Market Expansion
- **CDAP (Canada Digital Adoption Program):** Positioning Swift Spark as a solution for subcontractors to use with their $15k grant.
- **CanExport:** For future US/International expansion.

## 4. Special Case: No Wages for Founders (Current State)
- **Problem:** SR&ED refunds are based on salaries. No salary = No refund.
- **Immediate Strategy:** 
  - Switch focus to **Project-Based Grants** (e.g., Alberta Innovates Product Demo Program).
  - Use **Vouchers** to hire external consultants for specific tech gaps (Safe to claim 100% costs).
  - Keep full documentation for future salary conversion (Claim retrospectively).

## 5. High Impact Opportunity: Product Demo Program (PDP)
- **Goal:** Get $100k cash to fund a real-world test.
- **Requirement:** Partner with an Alberta Construction Subcontractor.
- **Action:** Find a "Friendly Subbie" and pitch a free digital transformation pilot funded by the province.
