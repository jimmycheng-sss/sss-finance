1. PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

The objective of this Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is to establish a collaborative framework for a Pilot Project ("The Project"). The Project aims to implement and validate the Provider’s proprietary Drawing Version Control Software ("The Software") within the Pilot Partner's manufacturing and installation workflows. The primary goal is to minimize costly production errors caused by version discrepancies and to streamline communication between the shop floor and field teams.

2. SCOPE OF THE PILOT PROJECT

  • Project Duration: Six (6) months, effective from May 2026 to October 2026.
  • Target User Base:
    • Approximately 300 Shop Workers (Manufacturing/Production floor).
    • Approximately 50 Site Installers (Field/Installation sites).
  • Deployment: The Software will be deployed across the Pilot Partner’s active projects during the duration of this MOU.
  • Hardware Policy: The Pilot Partner shall utilize its existing inventory of mobile devices or tablets to access the Software. The Provider will ensure Software compatibility and provide necessary technical configuration support.

3. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 Swift Spark Studios Ltd. (The Provider) shall:

  • Grant the Pilot Partner full access to the Software for the duration of the Project.
  • Provide comprehensive onboarding and technical training for the Pilot Partner’s staff.
  • Offer priority technical support to ensure minimal disruption to production.
  • Incorporate critical user feedback into the Software development roadmap.

3.2 Kingswood Interior Ltd. (The Pilot Partner) shall:

  • Integrate the Software into its daily operational workflows for drawing management.
  • Provide qualitative and quantitative feedback regarding software usability, bug reporting, and feature requests.
  • Collaborate in the collection of efficiency metrics (e.g., reduction in rework, time saved in drawing retrieval) to demonstrate the Project’s impact.

4. STRATEGIC COLLABORATION AND FUNDING

Kingswood Interior Ltd. acknowledges and agrees to be formally designated as the Primary Pilot Site for Swift Spark Studios Ltd.'s applications for government-backed technology grants, specifically the Alberta Innovates Product Demo Program (PDP) and other relevant federal/provincial subsidies.

  • No Direct Cost: Participation in this pilot is provided to the Pilot Partner at no direct software licensing cost.
  • Grant Support: The Pilot Partner agrees to provide a Letter of Support if required by grant agencies to validate the authenticity of the field trial.

5. DATA PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree that all technical specifications, business processes, and proprietary data shared during the Project shall remain strictly confidential. No data shall be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent, except for anonymized efficiency data required for government grant reporting.

6. NON-BINDING NATURE

This MOU is a statement of intent to collaborate and does not constitute a legally binding contract for long-term services, but rather a formal agreement to conduct the Pilot Project as described.
